www.fastfoodjobsuk.co.uk will also aggregate data that it collects from its registered users to identify employment trends across different industries, such as salary averages. This information will not be capable of being linked to individuals personally.

Your profile may be viewed by customers who are located in countries outside the European Economic Area.
